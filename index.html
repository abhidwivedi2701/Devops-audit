DevOps Audit

DevOps Audit is the measurement to find the state of DevOps Culture and Practice in a Software development environment. Since it is increasingly becoming part of the IT industrial cycle of software development, it ought to have a proper measurement to boost the product cycle by improving through required points generated after an Audit. It aims to benchmark the current DevOps Practices to industry best practices as per the DevOps Maturity Model.

Knoldus Audit Practice allows you to ensure that the product you are building has the right architecture, design, code quality, process for development, and a CI/CD enabled DevOps process. Our analysis and recommendations report makes you and your product successful.
